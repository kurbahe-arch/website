Our Story

A blend of tradition and modern comfort

Nestled in the lush green hills of Shillong, The Habitat is more than just a guesthouse. It's a sanctuary designed for travelers who seek peace, comfort, and a touch of local culture.

Our property features architecture that respects the local landscape while providing all the modern amenities you need for a relaxing stay. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or an extended exploration of Meghalaya, we provide the perfect base.

From our locally sourced breakfast to our curated travel recommendations, every detail at The Habitat is crafted to make you feel at home.
